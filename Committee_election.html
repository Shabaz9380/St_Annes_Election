Election Process

Phase 1: Announcement

Principal announces the Alumni Committee plan through school WhatsApp groups and notice boards, sharing objectives and responsibilities.

Phase 2: Nominations

5-7 day window for interested alumni to submit nomination forms with their vision and plans for their chosen role.

Phase 3: Verification

Principal and admin panel verify eligibility of all nominees and finalize the candidate list for each position.

Phase 4: Election Day

Electing of members by Principal & Teachers.

Phase 5: Results & Ceremony

Principal announces winners, officially forms the committee, and arranges oath-taking ceremony.

Phase 6: First Meeting

(Either online or offline)
Discusses yearly goals including alumni meet dates, fundraising plans, and community projects.
